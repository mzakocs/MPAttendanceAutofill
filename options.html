User Options

Start by filling out your information here. This is what will be auto-filled into your attendance forms.




Attendance Links

Put the URLs to your attendance forms here. This can be found by going into Schoology, going to your attendance form like usual, and copying the link near the top of your web browser.



Meeting Links

Put the URLs to your video meetings here. This can be found by going into Schoology, going to your Google Meet/Zoom meeting like usual, and copying the link near the top of your web browser.



Advanced Options

This allows you to create aliases for your classes. For example, a class can be labeled as "World History" instead of just "Period 1." In the fields below, input the names of classes you'd like to see appear instead of the generic period name.



Some teachers don't have permanent links for their Google Meet conferences. If you have one of these teachers, this option is perfect for you. In short, it allows for you to set a link to the homepage of Google Meets on your student account and then the extension will automatically fill out the meeting code for you.
There is a short video here that explains this feature better. Do not use this without watching the video first.
