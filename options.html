About

Welcome to MP Attendance Autofill! This is an extension focused on automating the attendance forms for Mountain Pointe High School. It's extremely annoying to have to go to the attendance form, fill out all of the fields by hand, and then remember whether you completed it already or not. This extension aims to get rid of all of these annoyances, and streamline the process for all Mountain Pointe students.

Setup

Let's get everything setup. First, start by filling out the "User Information" section. This asks for your First Name, your Last Name, and your Student ID. These are stored locally and used to fill out each individual attendance form. Second, fill out the "Class Information" section. This asks for all of your class periods, and allows us to fill out and keep track of each class's attendance form. To get your attendance form URL's, simply visit schoology, go to your attendance form like you were filling it out normally, and then copy the link at the top of the browser. This allows the extension to visit the attendance page and fill it out for you.

Once you've done both of those, you can start using the extension! Simply go to the extension menu in the top right of Chrome and click on the Mountain Pointe logo. This will open a menu where you can auto-fill a single attendance form, check if an attendance form has already been filled out today, or auto-fill all of your attendance forms for the day.
